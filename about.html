About Us

Our Mission

At Masvingo Chicks, our mission is to provide the highest quality chicks and eggs to farmers, ensuring they have the healthiest, most reliable products to support their poultry production.

We are deeply committed to our community, prioritizing strong relationships with local farmers and offering support at every step of the farming process. Our goal is to make poultry farming easier and more accessible for everyone, empowering farmers with the tools and resources they need to succeed.

Through dedication to excellence and service, we aim to be the trusted partner in every farmer's journey.

Our Products

At Masvingo Chicks, we offer a comprehensive range of products to support every aspect of poultry farming.

Our stock includes high-quality day-old layer chicks and broiler chicks, ensuring farmers receive healthy, robust birds for both egg and meat production. In addition, we provide essential chicken feed, pesticides, and vaccines, all designed to promote the well-being and productivity of your flock.

With our wide selection of poultry supplies, we aim to make farming operations smoother and more efficient, giving farmers everything they need to raise thriving chickens.

Our Distributors And Coverage

Located in the heart of Masvingo town, Masvingo Chicks is proud to work with three trusted distributors, N.RIchards, Fivet and FeedMix, who extend our reach across the town and its surrounding areas.

Our network of local partners also includes dedicated farmers who collaborate with us in testing products, growing chickens, and assisting in crucial research for our educational initiatives focused on poultry farming.

This partnership helps us continuously improve our offerings while providing valuable insights to local farmers.

Our coverage spans a 100 km radius around Masvingo, ensuring that our high-quality products and services are accessible to a wide range of customers in the region.
